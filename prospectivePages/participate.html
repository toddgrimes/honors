The Honors Program at UNA - What Works for You?

University Honors Program

UNA's campus-wide honors program is open to all majors and includes financial, academic, service, residential, and social components. For outstanding students who want the full honors package, this route may be for you.
Priority Deadline for Scholarships - December 15th, 2015 - Apply Online Today!

Take an Honors Class

Students with a 3.25 GPA or requisite ACT subscores (28 ACT in English for English Department classes; 27 Reading for History Department classes) can take Honors courses with the approval of the Director - a great way to get involved in a small class environment with awesome students!

Join the Honors Student Organization (HSO)

The HSO is our student led and student managed Registered Student Organization on campus. The purpose of the Honors Student Organization is to encourage connections between members of the university Honors Program, students enrolled in honors courses, and all students with a 3.5 GPA or better. This organization focuses on group leadership, campus involvement, activities, and community service. Activities of HSO include Step Sing, Homecoming, Intramurals, Lions Lend a Hand Service Day, Zombie Wars, Secret Pals, the Gatsby Formal, and much more.
