Scholarships

All students admitted into the Honors Program now qualify for in-state tuition. Students not admitted into the Honors Program but who receive at least $1000 in University scholarship funds also qualify for the in-state rate. Continuation of the in-state rate depends on adherence to the terms and conditions of the University scholarship award. For more information, please visit the Student Financial Services web site. All students in the Program receive an Honors scholarship based on class standing.

Scholarship Levels

Students in the first and second year of the Program typically receive a renewable scholarship of at least $300 per semester. Students in the third and fourth year of the Program typically receive a renewable scholarship of at least $600 a semester.

The Delores and Weldon Cole Honors Scholarships: Made possible by a generous gift from Delores and Weldon Cole (Class of 1960), these scholarships are available to current and prospective Honors Program students who demonstrate the highest levels of achievement. Students need only apply to the Honors Program to be considered for these awards.
The Virginia Bayless O'Connell Honors Scholarships: Named for Class of 1932 UNA alumna Virginia Bayless O'Connell, O'Connell Scholars are chosen from Honors Program applicants who demonstrate the highest levels of achievement. Students need only apply to the Honors Program to be considered for these awards.
Robert L. Potts and William G. Cale, Jr. Honors Scholarships: Established by a generous gift from Mr. Harry Smith, the Potts and Cale Scholars are chosen from Honors Program students who demonstrate a strong pattern of academics, community involvement, and service. Students need only apply to the Honors Program to be considered for these awards.
Grace and Howard Roberts Memorial Endowed Scholarship: Students need only apply to the Honors Program to be considered for these awards. Study Abroad ScholarshipsThe Honors Program offers when possible financial assistance to Honors students who study abroad. Please contact the Director for more details.

**************************************Please Note**************************************

All scholarship awards are subject to change depending on fund availability, budgetary constraints, and the size/quality of the applicant pool.For more information about Honors Scholarships, contact the Director. For more information about UNA academic scholarships, contact the Office of Student Financial Services.
