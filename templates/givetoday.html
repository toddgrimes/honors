10 Years of Excellence!

GIVE TODAY

During the 2015 - 2016 academic year, the Honors Program celebrated 10 years of excellence at UNA and reached an enrollment milestone of over 300 students. We want to thank all of our students, alumni, faculty, and donors. Because of your efforts and support the Program has made tremendous progress toward our primary goal of ensuring student success after graduation. The Honors Program continues to attract elite students from many places. Currently, we have students from Arkansas, California, Colorado, Florida, Georgia, Indiana, Kentucky, Michigan, New York, Ohio, Oregon, Texas, and Wisconsin in addition to our traditional core states of Mississippi, Tennessee, and Alabama. Our students are extremely active on campus, participating in student government, athletics, RSOs, Greek Life, residential advising, various ambassador programs, ROTC, LaGrange Society, and much more. Off campus they are just as active, presenting research at conferences, participating in internships in everything from finance to occupational safety, and volunteering for over 2500 service hours per semester. Additionally our students are representing UNA and the Honors Program on various alternative spring break programs and studying abroad in locations such as China, Germany, London, Paris, Peru, Costa Rica, New Zealand, and Cuba. YOU make all this possible! Thank you for your generosity and continued support.
