    Celebrating 10 Years of Excellence! The Honors Program is the ideal way for exceptional students to enjoy the high quality academic atmosphere at UNA. The Honors Program offers students a total undergraduate experience - academic, social, residential, and extracurricular. Over 90% of our graduates are employed in their degree fields or are pursuing graduate work. We want YOU to be a part of this special experience. Apply today! The Honors Program at North Alabama.
