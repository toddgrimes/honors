Welcome Prospective Students

Director's Greeting

Happy Anniversary Greetings from the Honors Program as we celebrate our 10th year of excellence and good times. The HP @ UNA offers students a total undergraduate experience—academic, social, co-curricular, extra-curricular, residential, and financial—of unsurpassed value. We are absolutely dedicated to your post-graduate success and to making your college experience personally rewarding and fun.

If you are ambitious to be the very best and want a support system that encourages success the HP @ UNA is for you. “Success” means different things to different people. We are here to help you define success on your own terms and attain it. 

The HP was founded in 2005, and we have continued each year since our inception to build on a foundation of outstanding academics and an enduring belief that the college experience should be unforgettable. Our primary mission is your success, but we are a young program, eager to try new things, and quick to keep in step with the pace of global change.

Explore our website to discover what you want to know about the HP @ UNA. Read what our students, past and present, have to say about the Program. Check out the benefits – and the requirements. We are looking for students who are ready to be something amazing. We think that may be you. 

Vince Brewton, Ph.D., Director
